# Market Conditions Rubric

Generates a scored market conditions rubric — 9 key dimensions rated 1–10 with clear criteria at each level and an overall composite reading.

## What it does

Produces a structured scorecard of current market health across dimensions like trend, breadth, volatility, credit conditions, and sentiment. Each dimension has explicit criteria for each score level, making the assessment reproducible and consistent over time.

## Trigger phrases

- "Score the market conditions"
- "Give me a market rubric"
- "Market health check"
- "Rate the current market environment"
- "Market conditions dashboard"
- "How does the market look right now?" (when the user wants a systematic breakdown)

## Output format

A scored table with dimension, score (1–10), and brief rationale, plus an overall composite reading.
